EDA and Visualization for Online Retail Sales

Conducted Exploratory Data Analysis(EDA) on online retail sales data and informative visualization using Tableau.

In this project, I successfully conducted an in-depth Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a dataset containing online retail sales data from an e-commerce company. Utilizing Tableau, a powerful data visualization tool, I created informative visualizations to gain valuable insights into sales patterns and product performance. The project aimed to support the company in making data-driven decisions and improving its overall sales strategies.


Data Source

The dataset used for this project was sourced from Kaggle : Online Retail Data Set
